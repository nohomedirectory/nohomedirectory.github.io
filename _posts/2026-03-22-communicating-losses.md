# Communicating Losses

11 PM, Sunday, March 22nd. At a lake somewhere in North Carolina.

I said I'd be transparent. The v1 spec decomposition and the resulting beads were shit. The 10k spec is intact, but the reduced artifacts I produced over the past couple days had significant information loss.

Something I've learned about this kind of work: the distance between momentum and paralysis is thinner than most people admit, and recognizing which one you're in is its own skill.

I'm at this lake, pacing around, looking at the stars, and redoing the spec decomposition right now with a better approach off my phone hotspot to my laptop.

Tomorrow's a different day.
